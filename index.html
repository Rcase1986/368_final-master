Robert Case

King of Kawaii

Hello my name is Robert Case, and the people of the internet haved dubbed me the "King of Kawaii" which means "king of cute." I am currently enrolled at Eastern Washington University to obtain a BA in Visual Communication and Design. Drawing is some thing I have enjoyed doing since I was in kindergarten and when I reached Junior High I realized it was some thing I wanted to do professionally. I have taught my self for many years how to draw with the computer and some my works have been the winners of contests and/or published in art books. I did some freelance for Wayforward Tech for their game "Shantae: Risky's Revenge," two my drawings where published in the "Street Fighter Tribute Art Book" and the "Mega Man Tribute Art book," I did a comic for a iPhone game called "Orezom Trails" and one of my drawings was shown at the 2013 San-Diego Comic-Con at a "Legend of Korra" panel.
